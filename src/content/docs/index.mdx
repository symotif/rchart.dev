## Features

<CardGrid stagger>
    <Card title="Open source" icon="pencil">
        Championed by the team at [symotif](https://symotif.com), all code is and forever will be available on [github](https://github.com/symotif/rchart) and protected by the [AGPLv3.0 software license](https://rchart.dev).
    </Card>
	<Card title="Physician-centered" icon="setting">
        Built by physicians, for physicians, rchart puts you and your workflow first. It will not act like a glorified cash register. Decide how you work best and [turn off those damn popups](https://rchart.dev) if you so choose to!
    </Card>
	<Card title="Portable" icon="open-book">
        Run rchart on [MacOS](https://rchart.dev), [Windows](https://rchart.dev), [Linux](https://rchart.dev), [Android](https://rchart.dev), [iOS](https://rchart.dev), [iPadOS](https://rchart.dev), and on the [web](https://rchart.dev)!
    </Card>
    <Card title="Modern" icon="open-book">
        Finally an EHR built for the 20th century... accessibility, theming, resizable, and yes, there is dark mode too.
    </Card>
	<Card title="Extensible" icon="open-book">
        Add community made [plugins](https://rchart.dev) with one click! Popular options include: [telemedicine](https://rchart.dev), [AI scribe](https://rchart.dev), [patient messaging](https://rchart.dev), and [handwritten notes](https://rchart.dev). For those interested in creating free or paid extensions, check out the [guide](https://rchart.dev) to get started!
    </Card>
	<Card title="Secure" icon="open-book">
        Fully HIPPA compliant and [externally audited](https://rchart.dev)!
    </Card>
    <Card title="Local-first" icon="add-document">
        Stop stressing about your internet connection and just sync to other nodes when you can. Learn more about [creating a network](https://rchart.dev) for your organization.
    </Card>
	<Card title="Free" icon="open-book">
        For those with DIY skills, download and run [rchart server](https://github.com/symotif/rchart-server) for free sync capabilities. Don't want to get in the weeds or need 24/7 support? Check our [pricing](https://rchart.dev). 
    </Card>
    <Card title="Global" icon="open-book">
        With over 10 languages supported and more coming (checkout our [roadmap](https://rchart.dev)), rchart is focused on helping doctors take care of patients, no matter what language they speak. Don't see your language supported? Help our [translators](https://rchart.dev) with your amazing skills!
    </Card>
	<Card title="Community-driven" icon="open-book">
        This project is built by community members across the globe. See the full list [here](https://rchart.dev)! and learn how you can [contribute](https://rchart.dev)!
    </Card>

</CardGrid>
